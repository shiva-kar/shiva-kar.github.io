Who I Am

Hi! I'm Shiva Kar, a BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) student with a deep fascination for how computers really work under the hood.

My journey began with C programming — learning pointers, memory management, and data structures from scratch. That foundation sparked my interest in systems engineering: operating systems, compilers, and computer architecture.

I enjoy working close to the hardware, writing programs that are fast and memory-efficient, and exploring the elegance of simple, well-structured code. When I'm not coding, I'm reading about computer science theory or contributing to open-source projects.
